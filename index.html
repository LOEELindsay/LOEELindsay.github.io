ABOUT

No matter what stage you are in your life, we are all on a journey and sometimes we become distracted and lose our way or just stop living because of fear, anxiety, addictions, hurt, unforgiveness, low self-esteem, shame, anger, regret, unrealized expectations etc. These and other issues of life pile on and clutter our hearts and we end up feeling stuck, not knowing how to find a way to move forward towards peace and healing.

The heart is the core of your spirit, the core of your soul and the core of your body.“Keep your heart with all diligence; For out of it are the issues of life.” Proverbs 4:23.

Journey to Kingdom offers soul care coaching using Biblical principles with a holistic approach to help you get at what is aching your heart; guiding, facilitating and equipping you with tools to unearth the issues that have been aching your heart. Resulting with an encounter with God, gaining clarity, healing, freedom, and wholeness, not only for your heart, but your body, soul and spirit.

You’re blessed when you get your inside world - your mind and heart - put right. Then you can see God in the outside world." Matthew 5:8

SOUL CARE COACHING

Soul Care is the ongoing work of clearing out the clutter in our hearts by collaborating and cooperating with God. Simply stated, soul care is operating in God’s best for you - - physically, emotionally, and spiritually. Soul Care, coaching helps you declutter, yield, surrender, heal your heart and soul and gain freedom to live a healthy, thriving, and victorious existence as a beloved child of God. and provides you with the tool to maintain your healing. Key areas of focus: clarity, identity, uncovering fear and God’s love. We serve anyone who is looking to heal and thrive.

What Will I Gain? Tools to maintain your soul care, healing, and an encounter with God in ways that will allow you to know and live in freedom, liberation, acceptance, pure love and joy. This can only happen if you're willing to invest in the coaching process, prepare, show up, participate, yield to God and put your discoveries into action, then breakthroughs, healing, and freedom will take place and you will see change.

If you're ready, click HERE to schedule a FREE 15-minute connect call so I hear your heart and concerns and we can determine if we are a mutual fit.
